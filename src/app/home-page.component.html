Hospital OPD Scheduling System

Social Distancing

We ensure that social distancing will be followed at hospitals by scheduling appoinments is such a way that the crowding at the hospitals will reduce significantly.

Isolation of COVID suspects

Our system generates alerts when the covid suspects are on arrival, we isolate him at the entrance itself to ensure safety of others. We guess if the visitor is covid suspect through the symptoms he is showing.

Walk-ins handled smartly

The walk-ins are handled smartly taking into consideration vacancies or by pushing time slots of next visitors to maintain social distance.

Offline Booking Facility

We provide an offline booking facility where the administrator does the booking on behalf of the visitor.

Keep visitors notified

Our algorithm does smart allocation of slots at the time of emergency at the hospital which keeps in mind social distancing protocols to ensure minimum number of visitors present in hospital premises.
